## Chrome Web Store Listing

**Title:** EtsyShield — Etsy Listing Compliance & SEO Scanner

**Short description (132 chars max):**
Protect your Etsy shop from suspension. Scan listings for trademark violations, policy issues, AND SEO optimization — all in one click.

**Detailed description:**
EtsyShield is the first Chrome extension that combines trademark compliance scanning with SEO listing optimization for Etsy sellers.

ONE CLICK, TWO ANSWERS: "Is my listing safe?" and "Will buyers find it?"

COMPLIANCE SCANNER:
• Checks titles, tags, and descriptions against 578+ trademarked brands
• Detects prohibited items and regulated claims
• Verifies handmade/originality policy compliance
• Flags digital vs. physical delivery conflicts
• Smart detection with confidence scoring — no false alarms

SEO ANALYZER:
• Title length and keyword optimization (140 char limit)
• Tag analysis (13 slots, duplicates, single-word detection)
• Description length and keyword placement
• Actionable fix recommendations for every issue

PRO FEATURES ($9.99/month):
• Unlimited scans (free: 5/month)
• AI Title Rewriter — 3 optimized, safe title suggestions
• AI Tag Generator — 13 SEO-optimized tags
• AI Description Improver — 300+ word optimized descriptions
• Appeal Letter Generator — AI-powered deactivation appeals
• Bulk Shop Scan — scan all listings at once
• Policy Change Alerts
• Scan History

PRIVACY: No data collection. All scanning happens locally in your browser. Zero tracking.
